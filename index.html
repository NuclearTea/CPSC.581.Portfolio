Michael's Gallery

In the journey to understand Michael, we created an application that helps you get some insight into Michael as well.

In a 30 Second chanllenge with hand-drawn details images, capture as many snippets as you can. Each image contains details and hints into Michael: his interests, likes, and experiences!

Bop It

Our Idea:

Proxemics

With the possibility of designing anything involving proxemics, I embarked on a journey to change the way we play music.

Using proxemics and id's, I map physical symbols to playing notes on a piano; allowing for an inifinte number of ways to play an instrument.
