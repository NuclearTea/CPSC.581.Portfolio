Michael's Gallery

Description

Imagine you had 30 seconds to learn about Michael. Gain the opportunity to take snapshots of his hand-drawn images which contain nuggets of his life and interests. Inspect the snapshots you take and try as many times as you want. You won't be able to catch everything the first time!

Initial Sketches

The following are some of my initial sketches (10 in total). They are a variety of different types of websites that attempt to highlight different aspects of Michael. In a sense, they are also a reflection, of my own journey on learning about Michael, his interests, his likes/dislikes and his overall lifestyle. In my initial meeting, I attempted to jot down the things that were important to Michael by asking about where he spends most of his time. In addition, I asked about fond memories he had and things he liked to do as a child. They vary from games to simple "discover something about Michael".

Sketch 1

The idea with this is that it was a discovery game where we would go on the journey to discover Michael's interests, similar to how I went a journey to discover his interests.

Initial Sketch 2

This introduces the use of a crosshair because I learned that Micheal likes photography. This is an important feature as this is something that we used in our final design. The user would be able to use the crosshair to move around and select different areas like music or books and learn about his favourites. This is also important as this idea is present in 1 of Michael's final drawings.

Initial Sketches 5/6

The top sketch highlights his room (which is dirty because Michael said he often procrastinates) and upon cleaning it, we learn about his favourite albums. This is idea is reflected in 2 of his hand-drawn images, one featuring his room, and the other featuring his favourite albums.

Initial Sketch 9/10

The top sketch is a drawing tool which would highlight Michael's interest of drawing.

The bottom sketch is a game which allowed the user to select multiple options which would increase or decrease his happy level vs energy level. This became one of the ideas which we decided to refine.

Refined Sketches

The following are refined sketches where we looked at all of our initial chosen where a combination of ideas that we all had which where a sketches (30) and then picked 2 ideas to focus and refine. The ideas picture taking app are related to the picture taking application.

Refined Sketch 5

The user would take pictures which would take you to special games that were things that Michael liked to do like rock climbing or walking in nature and store them in video experience galleries. This seemed a bit much storing the videos.

Refined Sketch 7

This was an idea which would allow the user to select some of Michael's favourite albums, and depending on how they made Michael feel, they would go to a snapshot taking mode where you could create a gallery of the background while the album played in the background.

Refined Sketch 8

This played of the idea of a gallery but rather than be albums, or nature, it was a table with his favourite food. This became one of the drawings that Michael eventually made.

Refined Sketch 9

This was an idea which allowed the user to take snapshots of his room which had different posters of what he liked like retro games (Mario, Final Fantasy, etc.)

Refined Sketch 10

This was the similar idea of a gallery but with a timer, I got this as inspiration from another team mate (Erich I believe).

Reflection

I was very pleased with the outcome of this assignment. I learnt a lot about the design process and I personally found the amount of sketching really valuable though quite difficult. I thought our idea reflected the personality of Michael in both obvious and subtle ways which was something that I was aiming for from the beginning. It really helps that Michael can draw!

I personally came up with some ideas regarding the photography and the gallery. I'm not necessarily sure who actually came up with the idea as we kind of talked and shared our ideas before all 10 sketches were finished and we influenced each other but I really like that we did that as it allowed me to gain different ideas and learn even more things about Michael. I also helped with development of the app.

Our process in deciding what we chose was just going through the ideas, picking apart what was similar and different as well as taking into consideration the difficulty of implementation. Overall, I am happy with the direction we went!
